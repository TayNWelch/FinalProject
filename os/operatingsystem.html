What is it?

The operating system (OS) is the interface your system will use to run applications. It acts as the middleman between the hardware of your PC and the software.


Popular brands and their prices

The most popular OS by a long shot is Windows. Windows 11 home costs $140 and more for the pro version.


Other things to consider

Picking between windows home and windows pro depends on what you will be doing with your PC. Windows pro is more geared towards businesses and work computers as it offers a little bit more security and a couple business oriented applications.
