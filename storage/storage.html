What is it?

The storage is where all your applications and software will be stored. There are two main components used for storage, SSDs and HDDs.

Popular brands and their prices

SSD Brands:

  • Samsung $35-$400
  • WD $30-$300
  • Crucial $65-$600

HDD Brands:

  • Seagate $60-$500
  • Toshiba $70-$400

Other things to consider

The main two things you want to consider are price and storage. SSDs tend to be more expensive for less storage while you can get terabytes of storage on an HDD for the same price or less. HDDs tend to be slower and are more prone to data loss and corruption. SSDs are considered way more reliable. HDDs also take up more room in the case. You also need to consider whether you want internal storage or external storage. External storage works the same as internal storage except it plugs into a USB port instead of being plugged into the motherboard. External storage can be very convenient if you move files around frequently as it can be unplugged and plugged into a new computer and be used immediately.
